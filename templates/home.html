WHY SAN ?

Natural Environment

San College of Arts and Science is surrounded by a beautiful natural environment that is both serene and inspiring. The campus is located in a lush green area, surrounded by trees.The air is fresh and clean, and the sounds of birds and other wildlife can be heard throughout the day. The natural environment provides a peaceful and calming atmosphere for students to learn, study, and relax in.

Cultural
Events

College of Arts and Science hosts a variety of cultural events throughout the year, providing students with opportunities to explore and celebrate different culturesfestivals and celebrations for cultural holidays such as Diwali, Eid, Christmas, and Chinese New Year. These events help to foster a sense of community and diversity on campus, and encourage students to appreciate and respect different cultures.

Industrial
Visits

San College of Arts and Science offers industrial visits to provide students with practical exposure to various industries. These visits are designed to help students understand the workings of the industry, gain insights into the processes involved, and witness real-world applications of theoretical concepts. The college collaborates with leading organizations in various sectors, such as manufacturing, technology, hospitality, and healthcare, to organize these visits.



NSS

San College of Arts and Science has a National Service Scheme (NSS) unit that offers students opportunities to engage in community service and social activities. The NSS unit aims to develop the personality of students through community service and encourages them to participate in social welfare activities. The unit provides a platform for students to develop leadership skills, teamwork, and social responsibility.

Sports

San College of Arts and Science offers a range of sports facilities to promote physical fitness and wellness among students. The college has a well-equipped sports complex with facilities for indoor and outdoor sports such as basketball, football, cricket, badminton, table tennis, and volleyball. The college also has a gymnasium with modern equipment and trained instructors to assist students with their fitness goals.

Transports

San College of Arts and Science provides bus transport facilities for students who commute to and from the college. The college has a fleet of buses that covers various routes across the city, making it easier for students to travel to the college from different locations. The buses are well-maintained and equipped with modern amenities such as GPS tracking and CCTV cameras to ensure the safety of the students.
