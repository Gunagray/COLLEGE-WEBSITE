Visit Our Campus

EXPERIENCE THE SAN NATURE


San College of Arts and Science is surrounded by a beautiful natural environment that is both serene and inspiring. The campus is located in a lush green area, surrounded by trees.The air is fresh and clean, and the sounds of birds and other wildlife can be heard throughout the day.

CAFETERIA


San College of Arts and Science has a spacious and well-equipped cafeteria that provides students with a wide range of food and beverage options. The cafeteria offers a clean and hygienic environment for students to relax and enjoy their meals.

LIBRARY


San College of Arts and Science has a well-stocked library that provides students with a wide range of books, journals, and other resources related to their academic pursuits. The library is spacious, air-conditioned, and provides a quiet and conducive environment for study and research.

HOUSING AND RESIDENCE


San College of Arts and Science provides hostel and residence facilities for students who require accommodation during their academic pursuits. The hostel is located on campus and provides students with a safe and comfortable environment to live in.
