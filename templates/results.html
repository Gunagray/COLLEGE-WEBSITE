rules and regulations

Students must have fulfilled the attendance requirements as per the university regulations. Typically, a student is required to have at least 75% attendance in all classes to be eligible to appear for the exam. Students must have completed all the coursework and assignments as prescribed by the university for the particular semester. Students must have paid all the fees and dues as per the university regulations before the deadline. Students must not have been found guilty of any misconduct or malpractice during the exams. Students must have passed the previous semester exams to be eligible for the next semester exams. It is important to note that these eligibility requirements may vary for different programs and courses offered by the university. Therefore, it is always advisable to check the specific eligibility requirements for your course of study on the official university website or consult with the university authorities for further information.
